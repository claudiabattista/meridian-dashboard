## Assumptions

- Work is performed remotely. No travel costs are included.
- Meridian will provide access to the repository and a point of contact (R. Tanaka's team) for R2 validation questions. Delays in access or feedback extending beyond 48 hours may affect the timeline.
- D1 (UI modernization) and D3 (dark mode) are not included in the fixed fee. If Meridian wishes to add them, we will provide a change order estimate at the Week 3 check-in.
- Scope is as described in the Technical Approach. If the R1 audit surfaces defects materially beyond what the RFP describes (e.g., requiring backend data model changes), we will notify Meridian before proceeding and agree on handling.

## Why Fixed-Fee

We are offering a fixed fee rather than T&M because Meridian has already had one vendor relationship end with unpredictable scope. A fixed fee gives J. Okafor's team a number they can approve and hold us to. Our risk mitigation is the Week 1 audit — we confirm scope before committing to the work, not after.
